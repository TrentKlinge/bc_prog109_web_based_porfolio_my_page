My Personal Interest

Outside of school I am very interested in music. I play both piano and guitar although I have played piano for much longer. On each instrument I like to play different kinds of music. On piano I usually play classical music. Some of my favorite classical composers are Frédéric Chopin and Claude Debussy. On guitar I like to play Country and Folk music. Some of my favorite Country and folk artists are Bob Dylan, Willie Nelson, and Towns Van Zant.
